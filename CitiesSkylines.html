Cities: Skylines

Game Overview

Cities: Skylines is a city-building simulation game developed by Colossal Order. Players take on the role of a city planner, designing and managing a city while balancing the needs of its citizens, infrastructure, and economy.





Game Details

  • Developer: Colossal Order
  • Publisher: Paradox Interactive
  • Release Date: March 10, 2015
  • Platforms: PlayStation, Xbox, PlayStation, Switch, PC
  • Genre: City-building, Construction and management Simulation

Game Review

Cities: Skylines is a city-building simulation game that allows players to create and manage their own cities. The game offers a wide range of tools and features for designing urban landscapes, managing resources, and addressing the needs of citizens. With its detailed graphics and complex simulation mechanics, Cities: Skylines provides an immersive experience for fans of the genre.

The game features a variety of scenarios and challenges, allowing players to test their city-building skills. The modding community has also contributed a wealth of user-generated content, enhancing the game's replayability and depth.
