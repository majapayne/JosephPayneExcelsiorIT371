NO MAN'S SKY

Game Overview

No Man's Sky is an action-adventure survival game developed and published by Hello Games. Players explore a procedurally generated universe, discovering planets, gathering resources, and building bases while encountering various alien species.





Game Details

  • Developer: Hello Games
  • Publisher: Hello Games
  • Release Date: August 9, 2016
  • Platforms: Xbox, PlayStation, PC
  • Genre: Action-adventure, Survival

Game Review

No Man's Sky is a wild ride from start to finish. It is mostly single-player, but incorporates multiplayer very well. I've spent many hours building bases, fighting epic space battles, fighting on random planets, completing the compelling stsory, and finding different ships. You can play the market to make money, spend the entire game building and creating structures and massive bases, play with other players, or just go throughout the game's story.
