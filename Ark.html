Ark: Survival Evolved

Game Overview

Ark: Survival Evolved is an open-world survival game developed by Studio Wildcard. Players must survive in a world filled with dinosaurs and other prehistoric creatures, gathering resources, crafting items, and building shelters.





Game Details

  • Developer: Studio Wildcard
  • Publisher: Studio Wildcard
  • Release Date: August 29, 2017
  • Platforms: PC, PS4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch
  • Genre: Survival, Action-Adventure
  • Modes: Single-player, Multiplayer
