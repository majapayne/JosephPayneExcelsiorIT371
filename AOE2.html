AGE OF EMPIRES II

Game Overview

Age of Empires II, also known as AoE2, is a real-time strategy game developed by Ensemble Studios and published by Microsoft. It was released in 1999 and is the second installment in the Age of Empires series.





Game Details

  • Developer: Ensemble Studios
  • Publisher: Microsoft
  • Release Date: September 30, 1999
  • Genre: Real-time strategy
  • Platforms: PC, Mac, Xbox

Game Review

Age of Empires II is widely regarded as one of the best real-time strategy games of all time. It features a rich historical setting, allowing players to control various civilizations from different eras. The gameplay is engaging, with a focus on resource management, city building, and military strategy.

The game offers a variety of campaigns and scenarios, each with unique challenges and objectives. The AI is well-designed, providing a challenging experience for players of all skill levels. The multiplayer mode adds even more depth, allowing players to compete against each other in epic battles.
