GRAND THEFT AUTO V

Game Overview

Grand Theft Auto V, also known as GTA V or GTA 5, is an action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It was released in 2013 for PlayStation 3 and Xbox 360, and later for PlayStation 4, Xbox One, and PC.




Game Details

  • Developer: Rockstar North
  • Publisher: Rockstar Games
  • Release Date: September 17, 2013
  • Platforms: PlayStation 3, Xbox 360, PlayStation 4, Xbox One, PC
  • Genre: Action-adventure, Open world

Game Review

Grand Theft Auto V is a masterpiece of open-world gaming, offering players an expansive and immersive experience. The game's three protagonists, Michael, Franklin, and Trevor, each bring unique perspectives and skills to the story, allowing for diverse gameplay. The sprawling city of Los Santos is a vibrant playground filled with activities, from heists to sports, ensuring that players are never short of things to do.

The graphics are stunning, with attention to detail that brings the world to life. The driving mechanics are smooth, and the combat system is engaging, making for thrilling action sequences. The online multiplayer mode adds even more depth, allowing players to team up or compete in various missions and activities.
